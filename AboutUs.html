Our Story

Basketball has always been more than just a game for Betty and Barbara, it was a passion that started on their neighborhood court. From early morning shootarounds to friendly competitions, the sport became a part of their daily routine and shaped their friendship. They realized that basketball was not just about scoring points; it was about teamwork, perseverance, and building a community where everyone felt welcome. Over the years, they developed a strong bond, learning valuable life lessons through every dribble, pass, and shot.

Inspired by their love for the game and a desire to share it with others, they founded Betty and Barbara’s Basketball Bonanza –a place where players of all skill levels can find the gear, training, and support they need to improve their game. Whether you're a beginner picking up a ball for the first time or a seasoned player looking to refine your skills, their mission is to make basketball accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone. Through their hard work and passion, they’ve created a space where basketball lovers can connect, grow, and pursue their dreams.

Early Days and Where we are now


What started as two best friends sharing a passion for basketball has grown into a thriving business that serves players across different levels and backgrounds. In the early days, Betty and Barbara traveled to small tournaments, selling basketball gear out of the back of their truck. Initially they were selling only the most basic equipment for casual players. Since the girls were still in college and were figuring out the business aspects, their parents didn't allow for them to travel places that were new. Hence, they were only allowed to operate in 3 college locations near them.

Over time, they decided to expand the business to target more serious players as well. Leaning in towards professional play we decied to align our services with the official National Basketball Association's regulations. From this, we managed to diversify our products while also improving the caliber we can provide, increasing options for the professional players. With the ties they had made over the years in prestigious colleges in Chicago, Lafayette and Indianapolis, they managed to become the lead providers for many varsity teams accross various states. And the growth has not stopped since! Chances are we will be opening a store near YOU very soon!
