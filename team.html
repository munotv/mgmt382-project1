Meet our Team

At Betty and Barbara’s Basketball Bonanza, we’re more than just a business—we’re a team of passionate basketball enthusiasts dedicated to bringing the best products and services to the game we love. Our leadership and staff are committed to excellence, innovation, and making basketball accessible and exciting for everyone. Get to know the faces behind the Bonanza!

Our Story

Founders and Owners


Betty Williams

Co-Founder & CEO

A former collegiate basketball player and lifelong fan of the game, Betty turned her passion into a business. With over 15 years of experience in sports management and retail, she leads the company with a vision to make basketball products and training accessible to players of all levels. Betty focuses on strategic partnerships, business growth, and product innovation.



Barbara Carter

Co-Founder & COO

Barbara brings her expertise in operations and logistics to ensure the smooth running of the Bonanza. With a background in sports marketing and event management, she oversees day-to-day operations, making sure every customer has an exceptional experience. Barbara also spearheads our community outreach programs, helping to bring basketball to underprivileged areas.

Management Team

  • Michael "Coach Mike" Thompson - Director of Training & Clinics
    A former professional player and current youth coach, Coach Mike runs our training camps, private coaching sessions, and skills clinics. With his deep knowledge of the game and a passion for teaching, he helps players refine their skills and reach their full potential.

  • Samantha Lee – Head of Product Development
    Samantha ensures that our basketball gear is of the highest quality. From designing custom basketballs to testing new training equipment, she works closely with manufacturers to bring innovative products to market.

  • David Richardson – Marketing & Community Engagement Manager
    David is the voice behind our brand, leading all marketing efforts, from social media campaigns to community events. Whether it’s launching new product lines or partnering with local schools and teams, David ensures our message reaches basketball lovers everywhere.
